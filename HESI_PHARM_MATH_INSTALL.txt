Add these files to your repo:

1. Put `hesi_pharm_math.json` into `/banks/` and rename it to:
   `banks/hesi_pharm_math.json`

2. Replace your current `banks_manifest.json` with:
   `banks_manifest.updated.json`

Why no `app.js` change was needed:
The current app loads banks dynamically from `banks_manifest.json`, so adding a properly-structured bank entry is enough for the new bank to appear in the dropdown.
